# Got to start somewhere...

...and appropriately for this blog it starts on the new year. I've set this blog up as an alternative to the instagrams and facebooks of this world as a place for me to share what I want to share. I'm thinking it'll mostly be images with a couple of words, sometimes with a purpose and sometimes just as a dump. I'll also write some stuff from time to time but as you can probably tell by now, I'm not a natural writer and this blog will probably wander around aimlessly until I have something more direct. Its probably giving year 7 English homework but for now I'm going to ramble on and try and put some thoughts (however stupid) on "paper". As a first stab I'm going to talk about the past couple of days.

From as long as I've been aware, New Year's Eve/Day has always been a family event for me. Always over to Aunts and Uncles or hosting the whole rabble at our family home (not that I did much or any of the hosting). This year provided a variation on that theme however this time with a different type of family: my dearest friends.


I count myself very lucky to have an irreplaceable group of friends from my childhood. To be born in the same time and place, and through the chaos of life come out 26 years later with an unbreakable attachment to each other still intact feels pretty special. Whilst we don't see each other half as much as we really should, the times we are all together are like we've never been apart.
